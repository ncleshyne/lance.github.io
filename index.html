Products

We offer a wide variety of brands such as Daikin, General Royal, Hitachi, and Xtreme. Below are some examples.

Daikin D-compact Series

Up to five selectable fan speeds, to enjoy precise comfort cooling at the fan speed that you desire. Inverter system ensures faster cooling with significant energy saving. The outdoor unit has a height of 420mm, which fits perfectly into existing built-in slabs for window-type airconditioners.

Daikin Cooling King Series

Cools the room faster. Gradual increase of set temperature for a comfortable environment. Sound level reduction from 48dBA to 23 dBA compared to window-type aircons.

Daikin D-Smart Series

D-smart King series is also available. Operates at 150 to 264 V and can withstand 0 to 440 V. Inverter system maintains consistent temperature for comfort all the time, especially when sleeping. Sound level from 51 dBA to only 21 dBA compared to window-type aircons.

Non-inverter type also available. Selectable Airflow Pattern: 360 Airflow/Circulation Airflow and Individual Airflow Direction Control. Suitable for high ceilings with two selectable temperature-sensors and 3 discharge modes.
